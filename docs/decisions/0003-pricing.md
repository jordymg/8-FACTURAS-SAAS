# ADR-0003: Launch price ARS $1,999/month, 250 invoices

**Date:** 2026-07-03
**Status:** Accepted

## Context
Experiment 1: two target users said yes without hesitation at $1,999/month. Adjacent market reference: Facturitas at ~$5,000/month (emission side).

## Decision
Single plan: ARS $1,999/month, up to 250 invoices/month, images and export included. Payment via Mercado Pago suscripciones.

## Alternatives considered
- Free tier — rejected for launch: Gemini cost per extraction, and price is already validated
- Usage-based pricing — rejected: kills the "barrera de entrada baja" simplicity

## Consequences
Simple pitch, protected margin via the 250 cap. Revisit tiers (accountant multi-client) post-MVP.
